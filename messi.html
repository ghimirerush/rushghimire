Lionel Messi (Lionel Andres Messi Cuccitini) is an Argentine professional soccer/football player who is plays for both Major League Soccer team Inter Miami CF, and the Argentina National Football Team. He is most known for winning the FIFA World Cup and being awarded eight Ballon d'Or Awards.



Early Life


Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987 in Rosario, Argentina. He was born into a soccer-loving family. His grandmother first introduced him to the sport, and the rest is history. As a child, Messi had a growth hormone deficiency, which caused him to be shorter compared to other boys his age. However, even with this weakness, he had incredible soccer skills and got recruited by the Argentine Team Newell's Old Boys at the age of 8.
